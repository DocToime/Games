Odd One Out

Score: 0

Mistakes: 0

Game Instructions

Objective:

Find the word or number that is the odd one out of a group of four.

How to Play:

  • Choose a Level and Round, then click on "Start Game" to begin.
  • Click on the word from each set that doesn't belong with the others.
  • Each correct answer increases your score; mistakes are counted.
  • At the end of each Round, a fun picture will pop up as a reward.

Features:

  • Difficulty Levels: Select different difficulty levels to match the player.
    • Beginner: Simple CVC (Consonant-Vowel-Consonant), CVCC, and CCVC words. Three will rhyme, and one does not.
    • Intermediate: Longer words, three of which belong to a category.
    • Advanced: Aimed at improving spelling and vocabulary using homophones and synonyms.
    • Expert: Less common words, also aimed at building vocabulary. Fun for all levels!
  • Font Selection: Choose a font to build familiarity with different writing styles. Andika is ideal for early readers, while cursive and OpenDyslexic fonts are also available.
  • Hint Button: Get clues when needed, as some questions are ambiguous or tricky. They are a good opportunity for more reading practice, so use liberally.

For Parents:

Encourage children to explain the connection between the three linked words. This helps develop critical thinking and language skills, and you may be surprised by their thinking!
