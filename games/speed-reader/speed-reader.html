Game Instructions

Objective:

Read and comprehend words or sentences quickly.

How to Play:

  • Choose a Level and Round, then click on "Start Game" to begin.
  • A word or sentence will flash on the screen. Pay close attention!
  • Answer a question related to the flashed content.
  • Each correct answer increases your score; mistakes are counted.

Features:

  • Adjustable Difficulty: Choose different levels for varied challenges.
  • Font Selection: Customize the font for a comfortable reading experience.
  • Hint Button: Get hints if you're stuck on a question.

For Players:

This game is designed to help improve your reading speed and comprehension in a fun and interactive way. Challenge yourself and see how quickly you can read and respond!
