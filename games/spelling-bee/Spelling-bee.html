Game Instructions

Objective:

Listen to the audio and spell the word correctly using the on-screen keyboard.

How to Play:

  • Choose a Level and Round, then click on "Start Game" to begin.
  • Listen to the spoken word and use the keyboard to spell it correctly.
  • Each correct answer increases your score; mistakes are counted.
  • At the end of each Round, a fun picture will pop up as a reward.
  • Use the "Hint" button to see the word used in a sentence.
  • Replay the word audio as needed by clicking the audio button.

Features:

  • Difficulty Levels: Adjust the difficulty level to suit the player's skill.
    • Beginner: Simple words, ideal for early spellers.
  • Font Selection: Choose from various fonts to assist with reading and familiarity.
  • Hint Button: Reveals a sentence with a blank where the word fits, to provide context and aid in spelling.
  • Audio Playback: Listen to the word again by clicking the audio button.

For Parents:

Encourage children to listen carefully and attempt spelling before using the hint. This helps develop listening and spelling skills.
